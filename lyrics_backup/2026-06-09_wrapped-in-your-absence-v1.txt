[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The night is quiet and the moon is low
I feel the fire in the undertow
My hands are empty but my blood is warm
I need your body in this coming storm

[Verse 1]
I kept the porch light burning through the rain
Watching the water on the windowpane
A pair of old boots sitting by the door
Remind me what I'm always waiting for

[Pre-Chorus]
The air is heavy with a slow desire
I feel the embers of a waking fire
Every shadow wears your silhouette
I want the moments that we haven't met

[Chorus]
Wrapped in your absence, craving your skin
Tearing the walls down let the wildness in
I feel the rhythm of an aching heart
Pulling me closer when we stand apart

[Post-Chorus]
I need you now, I need you near (Need you near)
Chasing the ghosts until you appear

[Verse 2]

Listening to the highway roll on by
The scent of cedar and a restless breeze
Are bringing all my senses to their knees
